MDNSResponder::stcMDNSServiceTxts

A list of zero or more MDNS TXT items. Dynamic TXT items can be removed by 'removeTempTxts'. A TXT item can be looke up by its 'key' member. Export as ';'-separated byte array is supported. Export as 'length byte coded' byte array is supported. Comparision ((all A TXT items in B and equal) AND (all B TXT items in A and equal)) is supported.
