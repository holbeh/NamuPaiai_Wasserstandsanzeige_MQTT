MDNSResponder::stcMDNSService

A MDNS service object (to be announced by the MDNS responder) The service instance may be '\0'; in this case the hostname is used and the flag m_bAutoName is set. If the hostname changes, all 'auto- named' services are renamed also. m_u8Replymask is used while preparing a response to a MDNS query. It is resetted in '_sendMDNSMessage' afterwards.
