Detailed Description

Public key decoder context.

The public key decoder recognises RSA and EC private keys, either in their raw, DER-encoded format, or wrapped in an unencrypted PKCS#8 archive (again DER-encoded).

Structure contents are opaque and shall not be accessed directly.
