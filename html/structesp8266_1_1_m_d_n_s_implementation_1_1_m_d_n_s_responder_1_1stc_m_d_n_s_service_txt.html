STRUCTSMDNSResponder::stcMDNSServiceTxt

One MDNS TXT item. m_pcValue may be '\0'. Objects can be chained together (list, m_pNext). A 'm_bTemp' flag differentiates between static and dynamic items. Output as byte array 'c#=1' is supported.
